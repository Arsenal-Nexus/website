Overview

The Arsenal Nexus RF Mapping platform integrates drone-based data collection with advanced signal-processing algorithms to produce detailed maps of radio-frequency environments. Whether you’re planning a new cellular deployment, troubleshooting interference, or surveying spectrum occupancy, RF Mapping gives you the insights you need—fast.

Key Features

  • Automated grid-flight paths for complete area coverage
  • Real-time signal-strength visualization on tablet or desktop
  • Frequency range: 400 MHz – 6 GHz
  • Onboard RTK GPS for centimeter-level geotagging
  • Customizable post-processing filters and export formats
