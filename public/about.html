About Arsenal Nexus

Learn about our journey, mission, and what drives us forward.

Denver-born and field-proven. We design RF mapping and mobile base station solutions to turn complex operations into confident decisions.

Our Story

Arsenal Nexus began in 2025 when a group of industry experts and college students came together with a vision to revolutionize drone technology. From a small group with a dream to working prototypes, our growth has been driven by curiosity, collaboration, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower organizations with cutting-edge drone solutions that enhance safety, efficiency, and data-driven decision making. We strive to bridge the gap between complex aerospace technology and real-world applications, delivering turnkey hardware and software packages backed by industry-leading support.

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by:

  • Innovation: Continuously pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

  • Integrity: Honest communication and ethical practices in all we undertake.

  • Excellence: Delivering reliable, high-quality products and services.

  • Collaboration: Building strong partnerships with customers and the community.
